About Me

I'm Danielle Kaneti, 23 years old, living in Moshav Sharona in the Lower Galilee.

I grew up with 3 siblings, 10 dogs, and a family ranch with about 100 horses in our backyard — a big part of who I am and where I come from.

Hobbies and Interests

Outside of my academic and professional life, I find joy in movement and balance.

I love dancing, working out, and of course — riding horses, which has been part of my life since childhood. These activities keep me grounded, energized, and connected to myself.

Education

Im am currently in my second year of studying Business Administration, specializing in Management Information Systems.

For my first year, I was honored to receive the President’s Excellence Award — something I never took for granted. I owe much of this achievement to the words of my brother that have stayed with me throughout: “What comes easy, goes easy. Everything takes persistence and hard work — and in the end it pays off. Those who stick with it, work hard, and never give up — get results.”

His voice became a reminder to keep going, and a source of motivation to stay focused and always give my best.
